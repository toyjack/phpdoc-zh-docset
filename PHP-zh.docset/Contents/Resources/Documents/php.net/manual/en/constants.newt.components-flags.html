constant meaning

NEWT_FLAG_DISABLED Component is disabled
NEWT_FLAG_BORDER  
NEWT_FLAG_WRAP Wrap text
NEWT_FLAG_NOF12 Don't exit form on pressing F12
NEWT_FLAG_MULTIPLE
